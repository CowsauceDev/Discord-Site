Open 24/7

Discord allows you to keep your online learning space open at all times, allowing students to join class without relying on clunky invites, converse about assignments and projects at any time, ask questions and attend office hours, and more.

Secure Quality

Discord and Classroom team up to provide best in class security and privacy and keep your learning environment safe from vulnerabilities in apps like Zoom, while still offering more consistent and clearer audio and video over fast and reliable servers.

Convenience

Start polls and send announcements, schedule reminders for classes and other events, test student knowledge, hold office hours, connect to your LMS, and so much more all from one simple, central tool already familiar to most of your students!
